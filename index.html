Welcome from Our President

Dear Students, Faculty, and Visitors,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to Averiq International University, where we have been fostering academic excellence and personal growth for over 30 years. Our commitment to providing a transformative educational experience is at the heart of everything we do.

At Averiq, we believe in empowering students to become critical thinkers, innovative problem-solvers, and engaged global citizens. Our distinguished faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and diverse community create an environment where students can thrive both academically and personally.

Whether you're a prospective student, current member of our community, or visiting researcher, I invite you to explore all that Averiq International University has to offer. Together, we are building a brighter future through education and discovery.

Dr. Charles Karanja
President, Averiq International University
